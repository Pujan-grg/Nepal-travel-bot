Name: Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple)
Location: Kathmandu
Type: Buddhist Stupa
Best Time: Early morning or sunset
Entry Fee: NPR 200 for foreigners
Description: Ancient religious complex atop a hill in the Kathmandu Valley. Offers panoramic views of Kathmandu city. Home to hundreds of monkeys.
Tip: Walk clockwise around the stupa, spin the prayer wheels.

Name: Boudhanath Stupa
Location: Kathmandu
Type: Buddhist Stupa
Best Time: Morning and evening prayer times
Entry Fee: NPR 400 for foreigners
Description: One of the largest spherical stupas in the world. UNESCO World Heritage site. Center of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal.
Tip: Visit rooftop cafes for the best views.

Name: Pashupatinath Temple
Location: Kathmandu
Type: Hindu Temple
Best Time: Early morning or evening aarti
Entry Fee: NPR 1000 for foreigners
Description: Most sacred Hindu temple in Nepal on the banks of Bagmati River. Dedicated to Lord Shiva. Non-Hindus cannot enter main temple.
Tip: Respectful dress required. Observe evening aarti from across the river.

Name: Pokhara Lakeside
Location: Pokhara
Type: Nature/Lake
Best Time: Sunrise and sunset
Entry Fee: Free (boat rides extra)
Description: Beautiful lakeside area with views of Annapurna range, Phewa Lake, and peace pagoda on hilltop.
Tip: Hire a boat at sunrise for mirror-like reflections of mountains.

Name: Chitwan National Park
Location: Chitwan
Type: Wildlife/National Park
Best Time: October to March
Entry Fee: NPR 1500 per day for foreigners
Description: UNESCO World Heritage site. Home to one-horned rhinos, Bengal tigers, elephants. Jungle safaris and canoe rides available.
Tip: Book a full-day jeep safari for best wildlife spotting.

Name: Lumbini
Location: Lumbini
Type: Religious/Historical
Best Time: October to March
Entry Fee: NPR 500 for foreigners
Description: Birthplace of Lord Buddha. UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient ruins, monasteries, and the sacred Maya Devi Temple.
Tip: Visit early morning when monks are praying for a peaceful experience.

Name: Bhaktapur Durbar Square
Location: Bhaktapur
Type: Historical/Square
Best Time: Morning to avoid crowds
Entry Fee: NPR 1500 for foreigners
Description: Ancient royal palace complex with stunning Newari architecture, 55-window palace, and pottery square.
Tip: Try the famous juju dhau (king yogurt) nearby.

Name: Sarangkot
Location: Pokhara
Type: Viewpoint
Best Time: Sunrise
Entry Fee: Free (transport extra)
Description: Famous viewpoint overlooking Pokhara valley and Annapurna mountain range. Popular for paragliding takeoff.
Tip: Drive up early morning for spectacular sunrise over the Himalayas.

Name: Sagarmatha National Park
Location: Solukhumbu
Type: National Park
Best Time: October to November, March to May
Entry Fee: NPR 3000 for foreigners
Description: Home to Mount Everest and other high peaks. UNESCO World Heritage site with unique Sherpa culture and wildlife.
Tip: Obtain TIMS card and Sagarmatha permit before trekking.

Name: Patan Durbar Square
Location: Lalitpur
Type: Historical/Square
Best Time: Morning or late afternoon
Entry Fee: NPR 1000 for foreigners
Description: Ancient royal square known for fine arts, Krishna Mandir, and traditional metalwork. Part of Kathmandu Valley UNESCO site.
Tip: Visit the Patan Museum inside the square for Newari art and history.
